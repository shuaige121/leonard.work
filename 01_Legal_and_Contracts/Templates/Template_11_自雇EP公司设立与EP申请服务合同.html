Article 1: Definitions 第一条：定义
1.1 Authorities & Decisions 主管部门与决定
"Authorities" refers to relevant Singapore government bodies (including ACRA, MOM, ICA, IRAS and banks, as applicable). Any approvals, registrations, pass issuance, renewals or refusals are solely determined by the Authorities.
“主管部门”系指新加坡相关政府机构（包括 ACRA、MOM、ICA、IRAS 及银行等）。任何注册、审批、签发、续签或拒绝均由主管部门独立决定。
1.2 Third‑Party Services 第三方服务
Certain deliverables may require licensed professionals (e.g., corporate secretary, registered filing agent, accounting, tax, legal) and/or banking partners. Such services are not provided as legal/tax advice by Party B unless expressly agreed in writing.
部分交付物可能需要持牌专业人士（例如公司秘书、注册申报代理、会计、税务、法律）及/或银行合作方参与。除非双方书面明确约定，乙方不提供法律/税务意见。
Article 2: Scope of Services 第二条：服务范围
2.1 Included Services 包含服务
Party B will provide the following (as applicable to Party A's situation):
(a) Needs assessment and feasibility discussion (business profile, role design, shareholding and timeline).
(b) Incorporation coordination: document checklist, name ideas screening, coordination with corporate service provider for filings and standard resolutions.
(c) EP application preparation support: document checklist, forms review, narrative/positioning suggestions, and submission coordination through the employer or appointed agent.
(d) Family planning coordination (if requested): Dependant Pass / LTVP route explanation and document checklist.
(e) Project tracking and status updates until an outcome is received.
乙方将向甲方提供以下服务（视甲方情况适用）：

(c) EP 申请材料协助：材料清单、表格与材料检查、文案与定位建议，并通过雇主账号或指定代理协调递交。

(e) 项目进度跟踪与节点提醒，直至获得阶段性结果。
